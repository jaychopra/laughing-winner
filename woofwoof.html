Pet Supplies Plus

designing a learning portal

Pet Supplies Plus is the third largest pet supplies retailer in America. I functioned as the lead UX Designer on a team tasked with improving an internal learning initiative.

THE CHALLENGE

Democratize the pet degree program by making it intuitive and accessible for all store members

Increase participation, retain members, and track progress nationwide
‍
Build stronger team member-manager relationships by recognizing pet degree achievements in stores

THE SOLUTION

Incentivize future employees by emphasizing the monetary-compensation of the Pet Degree Program in job postings

Culture Change: Incorporate Pet Degree achievement into existing in-store cultural practices to raise awareness amongst team members

Design: Develop an interface for team members to take responsibility for their own respective Pet Degrees
